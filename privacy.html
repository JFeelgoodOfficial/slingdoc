Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

This is a starter policy. Before launch, have a privacy attorney review it against your actual data practices, CCPA/CPRA, and any state-level employment-data requirements in jurisdictions where you operate.

Who we are

SlingDoc is a product of Core Goals Consulting, based in San Antonio and Austin, Texas. You can reach us at hello@slingdoc.com.

What we collect

  • Worker-submitted data: phone number, SMS text content, photo attachments (badges, documents), and timestamps.
  • Manager / customer data: name, work email, company, and dashboard activity logs.
  • Operational data: routine server logs and aggregated usage statistics.

How we use it

We use collected data to operate the SlingDoc service, deliver time-and-attendance records to your designated payroll/HR systems, support your account, and improve reliability. We do not sell worker data.

Where it's stored

Data is stored on AWS infrastructure with 99.99% durability. Access is restricted to authorized personnel and your designated managers.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by emailing hello@slingdoc.com.
